Overview


        This page is especially created for ESL students of grade 10th to help them improve their Reading skill. The content relates to the topic Films and cinema. Our website includes 4 parts:     

          Matching: which helps students to master the meaning of some new words in the passage, this also broadens students' vocabulary.
          Multiple-choice: students will get general understanding of the content after reading passage in text book.
          Crossword: in this task student can learn more word about kinds of film and feelings.
          Cloze test: this task helps students develop reading comprehension and students will get general knowledge about "cartoon film".

         The tasks in our page are at the adequate level for students to practice reading at home. Students may find some interesting things on this page and may also see that reading and learning vocabulary can be exciting.
         OBJECTIVES: By the end of the module, students will be able to:

        - Master some words related to film topic.
        - get more understanding about film: some kinds of film, and film's history as well
        - Guess word's meaning from context
        - Read to get specific information to answer the question
        - Enjoy learning English with software.


     TARGET AUDIENCE: EFL Students of grade 10th.
     LANGUAGE LEVEL: Grade 10th.
